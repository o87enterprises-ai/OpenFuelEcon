L/100km = 235.215 ÷ MPG

MPG = 235.215 ÷ L/100km

What does this mean?

MPG (kilometers per gallon) is used in the US. L/100km is used in most other countries. Lower L/100km is better; higher MPG is better.

This conversion uses the standard factor of 235.215.
